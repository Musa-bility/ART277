About Us

GATEWAY FOUNDATION was founded in March 2022 and carries out a mission to provide health care, educational assistance and empower the most needed people in Liberia and other parts of Africa. We provide educational assistance for at-risk and less privileged children in diffrrent High schools and Universities in Liberia. We also provide for people with all kinds of disabiliies their basic need in order to enable them live like other normal people around them. We've partnered with nonprofits in 50+ countries who work on the ground and post the projects that their communities really need. provides a wide range of projects to appeal to a large section of charitable people who want to feel they are contributing in some way to improve the lives of those less fortunate than them.

Our Vision

We see equal value in all lives. And so we are dedicated to improving the quality of life for individuals around the world. From the education of students in liberia, to the health of a young mother in Nigeria, we are catalysts of human promise everywhere.
